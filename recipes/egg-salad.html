Egg Salad

    I think we all too-often forget about classic egg salad! It’s such an easy recipe to make and is something you can pull from the fridge at a moment’s notice to eat by itself or make sandwiches or lettuce wraps.

    Feel free to add some fresh lettuce, arugula, sliced tomato, avocado, and crispy bacon to those sandwiches to take things up a notch!

  1. How to Make Egg Salad

    2. See recipe card below this post for ingredient quantities and full instructions.

    • Hard boil and peel the eggs (as outlined above).
    • Slice them in half lengthwise and use a small spoon to remove the yolks.
    • Dice up the egg whites into small squares of even size
    • Add the vinegar to the warm egg yolks and let them cool.
    • Incorporate the mayonnaise, relish, mustard, dill, paprika and garlic salt.
    • Mix until well-combined, then gently stir in the onions, celery, and egg whites.
    • If possible, chill for at least 30 minutes prior to serving.

  2. Make Ahead Method

    3. Assemble the egg salad as outlined and transfer to an airtight container. Refrigerate for up to 2 days prior to serving.
